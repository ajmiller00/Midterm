History

Rêve au Chocolat was was founded in 1987 by French chocolate connoisseur Vail Des Meaux. After stumbling into a chocolate making course at the age of eighteen, Meaux became immediately fascinated by the art of chocolate. Just three years later, following professional culinary training at Ecole Chocolat, Meaux opened Rêve au Chocolat.


Rêve au Chocolat was originally located in Kendall Square, but has since expanded in size and moved to Harvard Square.

Our method

For more than three decades, Rêve au Chocolat has provided Boston with the finest chocolates. Never using molds, each of our chocolates are handcrafted over a three-day period. The unique flavor and beautiful presentation are our great pride and joy.
